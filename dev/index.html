Qdo

by jpco

Log in, sign up, or try the app anonymously.

Qdo (or qto.do) is a simple—to the point of being ugly and nearly featureless—to-do list web app.

It presents your outstanding items in two forms: First, in a tree structure where you can split and group items into nested structures; and second, in a queue where you can set up your most immediate items to be done.

Not recommended for actual use.
